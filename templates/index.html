About ChatGroq Bot

ChatGroq Bot is your personal data science assistant powered by artificial intelligence. It uses advanced natural language processing (NLP) techniques to understand and answer questions related to data science concepts, machine learning algorithms, statistical analysis, and more!

How it works: When you ask a question, the bot processes your input, searches for the most relevant information from its knowledge base, and provides a response. It continuously improves its understanding through interactions and can assist with explaining complex data science topics in a simple and understandable way.
